SIGIRIYA



Introduction


Unbelievable technical, stone methods, wall Arts with beautiful environment shows you the unlimited talent of the Sri Lankan aborigines. This world historical monument located at Mathale District of Sri Lanka. According to the Sri Lankan history, the king Kashyapa created this Lion's rock for hide from his brother's attacks. After the king's death the Sigiriya named as a Buddhist monastery until 14th century.

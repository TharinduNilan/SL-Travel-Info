Introduction


Yala National Park is the second-largest national park in Sri Lanka. The park is located about 300km from Colombo, the capital of Sri Lanka. Yala belongs to the Uva and Southern Provinces of Sri Lanka. The park is divided into 5 sections and sections 1 and 2 are reserved for the public.

This park is a large park spread over the southwest. It covers an area of about 979 square kilometers in both the Uva and Southern Provinces. However, only 141 square kilometers of land have been set aside for the public.

Access The Park

  1. From Colombo to Hambantota Tissamaharama Kirinda Road and turn off at Yala Junction Palatupana Park Headquarters is one access road.
  2. Colombo Ratnapura Embilipitiya Suriyawewa Mirijjawila via Hambantota or
  3. Colombo Ratnapura Udawalawe Thanamalwila Tissamaharama Yala Junction
  4. You can also reach the park from the Thanamalwila Lunugamvehera junction by coming to Kataragama over the reservoir dam.
  5. Mahiyangana Bibila Buttala Sellakataragama Katagamuwa is also one of the access roads.
